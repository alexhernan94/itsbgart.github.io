Privacy Policy for Anailica

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

Anailica ("we", "our", or "the App") is a personal analytics tool developed for a single content creator. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle, process, and protect data retrieved from your TikTok account via the TikTok API.

1. Information We Collect and Access

Our App accesses only the minimum necessary data from your authenticated TikTok account to perform personal analytics. This includes:

  • Profile Information: Basic public profile statistics (such as follower count, profile picture, and username).
  • Content Data: Your public video list, upload history, and video-specific engagement metrics (views, likes, shares, comments).

2. How We Use the Information

The accessed data is used solely to provide personal dashboard metrics and insights to you, the authenticated user. Specifically, it is used to:

  • Track your content performance over time.
  • Identify which content formats generate the most engagement.
  • Monitor audience growth trends.
  • Generate data-driven content strategy recommendations using AI.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store your data. All retrieved data from the TikTok API is fetched dynamically and processed during your active session. We do not maintain any persistent databases or external storage containing your personal or TikTok account metrics.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties. Since this is a personal analytics tool, only you (the authenticated owner of the account) have access to the dashboard and the metrics processed during your active session.

5. Security

We are committed to securing your data. Access to the TikTok API is managed securely using OAuth 2.0 protocol standards. The communication between your browser, our application, and the TikTok API is fully encrypted using HTTPS.

6. User Control and Revocation of Access

You have full control over your data. You can disconnect the App and revoke its access to your TikTok account at any time. To do this, go to your TikTok Account Settings -> Security and login -> Manage app access, and remove permissions for Anailica.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer directly.
